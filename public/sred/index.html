Contractor Misclassification

Developers billed as contractors are excluded from SR&ED. Most companies bleed eligible spend here without realizing it. Restructuring the engagement model unlocks it.

🌎

IP in the Wrong Entity

If your IP lives in a US parent or holding co, SR&ED eligibility evaporates. We restructure R&D entity placement so the Canadian entity owns what CRA needs to see.

📈

Grant Stacking Gaps

SR&ED, IRAP, provincial credits, and NRC programs can compound — but most companies claim only one. We map the full incentive landscape and stack what's eligible.

🏢

Wrong Corporate Structure

CCPC status, associated company rules, and intercompany agreements determine refund size. A 15-minute structure review can be worth $200K+ in recovered refundability.
