About EXECOM

The SR&ED industry optimizes claims.
We optimize companies.

EXECOM was built by founders and deal architects who saw that the entire SR&ED consulting industry was solving the wrong problem.

The industry is solving the wrong problem

Every SR&ED firm in Canada competes on three things: eligibility identification, claim preparation, and maximizing refund percentage. That's the entire pitch. And it's the wrong frame.

The claim is the output. The structure is the input. And most companies — including their SR&ED consultants — never look at the structure. They don't ask where the IP lives, whether contractor arrangements are killing eligibility, whether IRAP and provincial credits are stackable, or whether the corporate design itself is leaking refundability.

The result: most Canadian tech companies capture only 40–70% of the non-dilutive incentive value their R&D activity actually generates. The rest evaporates through structural neglect.

We built EXECOM to fix the structure first, then file the claim. The claim gets bigger. The fees get smaller. And the non-dilutive layer of the capital stack grows year over year.
