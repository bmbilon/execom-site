Industries

Every vertical has different
structural challenges

The structural decisions that affect SR&ED eligibility vary by industry. Contractor models, IP arrangements, entity structures, and available grant programs differ across verticals — and so do the optimization opportunities.

💻

Software & SaaS

Contractor and offshore development teams frequently create eligibility gaps. We restructure engagement models so the full R&D workforce contributes to eligible expenditure.

Common structural issue: contractor classification
🤖

AI & Machine Learning

Complex IP ownership questions, cloud compute classification, and dataset licensing create structural challenges that affect both eligibility and audit defensibility.

Common structural issue: IP ownership architecture
🔧

Hardware & IoT

Prototyping, materials R&D, and cross-border supply chains create IP placement issues. Intercompany agreements with manufacturing partners often limit credit eligibility.

Common structural issue: cross-border entity design
🌱

Cleantech & Energy

Dense grant landscape (SDTC, IRAP, provincial green funds) that compounds when stacked with SR&ED. Most cleantech companies claim only one layer of available programs.

Common structural issue: grant stacking gaps
🦾

Robotics & Automation

Strong technological uncertainty creates eligible claims, but intercompany agreements with manufacturing partners and contractor arrangements frequently limit actual credit value.

Common structural issue: intercompany agreements
🧬

Life Sciences & Med-Device

Rigorous documentation translates into strong SR&ED evidence — when the entity structure is right. Holding co / opco splits frequently cause refundability problems.

Common structural issue: entity refundability
🌾

AgTech & Food Science

Overlapping federal and provincial agriculture R&D programs that compound when stacked correctly. Many ag companies don't realize SR&ED applies to their field trials and process R&D.

Common structural issue: program eligibility awareness
🎮

Gaming & Interactive Media

Engine development, procedural generation, and rendering R&D are highly eligible but frequently underclaimed. OIDMTC stacking adds a significant layer most companies miss.

Common structural issue: incentive stacking
📡

Telecom & Connectivity

Network infrastructure and protocol R&D with established SR&ED track records. Cross-border entity structures between US parent and Canadian opco need careful IP placement design.

Common structural issue: IP placement
